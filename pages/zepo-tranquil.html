Tranquil Theme

The Project

Between Jan 2015- Sep 2015, I worked at Zepo Technologies Pvt. Ltd, a platform that allowed customers to set up their online store within 5 minutes. During my stay we were assigned to work for theme development, which allowed customers completely customizable and flexible themes. Tranquil was the first to be introduced, an easy to navigate, rich in feature and modifiable theme, where it adapted well to stores comprising of 100's to 1000's of products.

Visit the website Tranquil Theme

PALETTE

TECHNOLOGY

E-Commerce

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap 3.0

Features

An integral User Experience within an e-Commerce website, would most of the times be during the Checkout. Here, a users interaction is of most importance, to ensure they can clearly understand what they would like to purchase, their address, the total amount and how they would like to pay. Tranquil has ensured this process is as fluid as possible, by providing simple and sophisticated features, allowing the user to navigate with only the click of a button.
