Ropeway India

The Project

A one page website for a Ropeway & Tourism Consultancy Service company based out of New Delhi, India. The website focuses on showcasing their work through photos, elaborate service messages, a visual map and a timeline carousel. The carousel is a mix of both personal and professional content. One of the best features is the logo which represents the company, eco-tourism and India.

Visit the website ropeway.in

Features

While designing the website, I knew that The Map would be one of the best concepts to represent the body of work by the company, in a direct, simple and summarized format. Visualizing data is key to quickly understanding and getting answers for numerous questions a user may have. While looking at the map it is clear that most of the work has been done within central and northern India, along with short descriptions that help in describing the type and amount of work done.
